About Izzy & Visual Visage

Visual Visage is a collection of artwork, design, and creative expression — built with the goal of turning everyday ideas into meaningful visuals.

From posters to photography, films to print designs, this is the space where passion meets purpose and each piece tells a story.

Every project is made with intention and curiousity. Welcome to the portfolio — and thank you for being here!

Izzy is a creative in the East Coast with the a background in music and arts. He has a passion for storytelling through visuals. When not creating, you can find him exploring nature, skateboarding, or playing music.
